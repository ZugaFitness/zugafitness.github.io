Common Questions

How do the live classes work? +

All sessions run on Zoom. After signing up you receive a link and your monthly schedule. Classes are capped at 10 students so every person gets real-time attention from the instructor.

Do I need any experience? +

No. Several of our 20+ monthly classes are designed specifically for beginners. Just show up with a mat and comfortable clothing.

Which timezones do you cover? +

We run classes daily across IST, GMT, EST, GST and PHT. You’ll receive the full schedule after signing up.

Can I switch between yoga styles? +

Yes — Growth plan members get access to all 8 styles across all 5 timezone slots. Starter plan members choose one style per month.
