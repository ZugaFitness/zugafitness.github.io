Common Questions

Do I need prior yoga experience? +

Not at all! Our group classes are designed for all levels. Our instructors provide modifications for beginners and challenges for more advanced practitioners.

What if I miss a class? +

Since these are live sessions, we encourage consistency. However, if you miss a session, you can join any other scheduled group class within the same week.

Which time zones are classes held in? +

We currently offer slots across IST (India), GMT (UK), EST (US/Canada), and GST (UAE/Dubai) to ensure you can practice at a convenient time wherever you are.

What do I need to join? +

A yoga mat, comfortable clothing, a stable internet connection, and the Zoom app installed on your laptop, tablet, or phone.
