Common Questions

What technical setup do I need? +

Just a device (laptop, tablet, or phone) with the Zoom app installed and a stable internet connection. We recommend a laptop for the best experience so you can see the instructor clearly.

Are these sessions pre-recorded? +

No. All our sessions are 100% live. We believe in the power of real-time connection and interaction between students and instructors.

How many people are in each class? +

We cap our virtual group sessions at 10 screens. This ensures that every participant gets individual attention and feedback from the instructor.
