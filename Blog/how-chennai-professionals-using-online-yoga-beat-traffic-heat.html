The Chennai Commute Problem: OMR Traffic + 40°C Heat = Exhaustion

For those living in T Nagar or Adyar and working in Siruseri, the daily commute is a test of endurance. Traffic in the IT corridor averages 1.5 to 2 hours daily, often under a sun that pushes temperatures past 40°C during the peak summer months. By the time an IT professional reaches home, they are often dealing with "heat stress"—a combination of physical dehydration and mental fatigue caused by extreme temperatures and long hours in transit.

Traditional fitness routines often require another commute to a local studio, which only adds to the exhaustion. Practicing online yoga for chennai professionals allows you to skip the road entirely. Instead of fighting for a parking spot or sitting in an auto-rickshaw in the humidity, you can transition from your laptop to your yoga mat in seconds.
