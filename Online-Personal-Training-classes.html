Common Questions

How is personal training different from group classes? +

Unlike group classes where the sequence is fixed for everyone, personal training is 100% customised. Your instructor monitors your form every second, adjusts the pace based on your energy levels that day, and focuses specifically on your health goals (like back pain or PCOD).

What if I have an injury or medical condition? +

This is where personal training shines. We specialise in therapeutic yoga for injuries, PCOD, and postpartum recovery. Your instructor will work with you (and your doctor's advice) to provide safe, effective modifications.

How do I pick my session time? +

During your free consultation, we'll discuss your preferred timing. We offer flexible slots across all time zones to ensure your practice fits seamlessly into your life.

How long before I see results? +

Most students feel a difference in their energy and flexibility within the first 2-3 weeks. For specific health goals like weight loss or hormonal balance, we typically see significant progress within 8-12 weeks of consistent practice.
