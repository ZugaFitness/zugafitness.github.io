Common Questions

What's the difference between Hatha and Vinyasa? +

Hatha yoga typically focuses on individual poses with a slower pace and longer holds, while Vinyasa yoga is more dynamic, linking poses together in a continuous flow of movement with breath.

Can I join if I've never done yoga before? +

Yes! We offer classes suitable for all levels, and our instructors provide modifications to ensure everyone can practice safely and comfortably.

What do I need for the class? +

A yoga mat, comfortable athletic wear, and a stable internet connection for the Zoom session. Props like blocks or a strap are optional but helpful.
