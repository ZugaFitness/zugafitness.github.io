Common Questions

Do I need any yoga experience for Pranayama? +

No experience is needed. Pranayama is a seated practice that can be performed by anyone, regardless of their flexibility or prior yoga knowledge. Our instructors guide you through every step.

What time of day is best for these sessions? +

Pranayama can be practiced at any time, but it's especially effective in the morning for clarity or in the evening to wind down for sleep. We offer sessions across multiple time zones to fit your schedule.

Can Pranayama help with anxiety or insomnia? +

Yes, absolutely. Techniques like Nadi Shodhana and Box Breathing are scientifically proven to activate the parasympathetic nervous system, reducing cortisol and helping you fall asleep faster.

What do I need to join — any equipment? +

You don't need a yoga mat or special gear. Just a quiet space, a comfortable chair or floor cushion, and earphones to hear the instructor clearly.
