Common Questions

Do you travel to all areas of Bangalore? +

Yes — we cover all areas of Bangalore. No travel surcharge within city limits.

What space do we need at our office? +

Any open area works — a conference room, terrace, lawn, or cleared office floor. We just need enough space for participants to lie down.

What do employees need to bring? +

Nothing — just comfortable clothing. We bring any props needed.

Can the session be customised for our team? +

Yes. Share your team size, any specific health considerations, and your goals — we'll tailor the session accordingly.

Why is it "Corporate Yoga Week"? +

Since International Yoga Day falls on a Sunday this year, we've extended our onsite availability to June 16–25. This allows your team to participate during regular work hours on a weekday that suits your schedule best.
