The virtual session includes a 60-minute live interactive session via Zoom/Teams for up to 30 participants, time-zone smart scheduling (IST, GMT, EST, GST), and a post-session wellness report.

Currently, our onsite sessions are exclusively available in Bangalore, covering areas like Whitefield, Koramangala, HSR, Indiranagar, Bellandur, and Sarjapur. No travel surcharge within city limits.

Can we mix onsite + virtual participants? +

Yes, we offer a hybrid option where your onsite Bangalore floor session can be live-streamed for your remote global team members simultaneously.

Onsite sessions accommodate up to 50 participants. Virtual sessions are optimized for up to 30 participants to ensure high interaction quality. Larger groups can be accommodated with a custom quote.
