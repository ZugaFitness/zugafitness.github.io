Online Yoga FAQs

How do your live online yoga classes work?

All classes run live on Zoom. After booking, you receive a Zoom link and class schedule. Sessions are capped at 10 students so every person gets real-time corrections from the instructor.

Do you have online yoga for beginners?

Yes — several of our 20+ monthly classes are designed specifically for beginners. No prior experience needed for Group Classes or Pranayama sessions.

Can I join classes anytime?

We run classes across IST, GMT, EST, GST and PHT timezones. You'll receive the full schedule after signing up.

What equipment do I need?

A yoga mat and comfortable clothing. For Pranayama, just a quiet space and earphones — no mat required.

Do you offer one-on-one virtual yoga sessions?

Yes. Our Personal Training programme offers fully personalised 1-on-1 Zoom sessions built around your body and goals. Start with a free consultation.

OUR MISSION

Yoga Without Borders

We built Zuga because great yoga shouldn't depend on where you live. Whether you're on a Bangalore terrace at 6am or a New York apartment at 7pm EST — you deserve a live instructor who knows your name, tracks your progress, and challenges you to grow.

Since 2023, we've brought together students from India, the US, UK, Canada and UAE under one roof — a virtual studio with the warmth of a neighborhood class and the reach of a global platform.

For our Bangalore community, we also bring certified instructors directly to workplaces and apartment communities — serving Whitefield, Koramangala, HSR Layout, Indiranagar, Bellandur and Sarjapur Road.
